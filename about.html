Our Story

Taxify was started as a solution for smaller businesses that struggle to afford certified public accountants, and as a result suffer from a lack of information that they could have been taking advantage of.

Akhil possesses expertise with startups as well as larger companies, providing a precise understanding of the problem. Christopher has experience in software development and an interest in Artificial Intelligence. The two took inspiration from softwares such as QuickBooks by Intuit, and realized that these softwares do not provide information on codes and loopholes in their tax management structures. Although some softwares do have chatbots, a critical issue is that they are not precise or detailed enough to handle decisions with accuracy, leaving many unaware of a potential to save money that would be crucial towards their lives or businesses.

Taxify aims to optimize entire industries. In the future, we hope to create solutions that clear many companies' biggest hurdles.
